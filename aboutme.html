About Me

I was born in St Thomas hospital in London. I am of Jamaican heriatage and I am the oldest of 3 siblings.

Key Achievements & Interests


I am a very active person and I enjoy playing football and going to the gym. I have played football for most of my life and had some great experiences playing and different levels and meeting many different people. I had a lot of success playing at youth level and represented some great teams such as Charlton Athletic, QPR and Crystal Palace. I am a liverpool fan and I am really enjoying the teams resurgence at the moment!

I also really enjoy Athletics and also represented my school and my borough at the London youth games where I placed 2nd In the 100m sprint. that was one of my proudest achievements as I was able to say that I was the 2nd fastest in london for my age.

When I'm not being active I am usually doing something creative and I really enjoy watching shows or videos on how things are made or how things work and I like trying to test things and find out myself.
